                        <p>Written by Peter David, with layouts by Daniel Govar and art by Rock-He Kim, the CAPTAIN AMERICA: THE WINTER SOLDIER PRELUDE Infinite Comic&mdash;<a href="http://comicstore.marvel.com/Marvels-Captain-America-The-Winter-Soldier-Infinite-Comic-1/digital-comic/32593" target="blank">available now</a>&mdash;bridges a divide in the Marvel Cinematic Universe. Rosemann then tapped David, who previously adapted &ldquo;Captain America: The First Avenger&rdquo; as a comic, to headline this latest project.</p>
                        <p>Here, David explores a key theme of Steve Rogers&rsquo; cinematic journey: the evolution of his patriotic zeal, from the scrappy young man bent on joining the war effort to a more thoughtful leader ever mindful of his minders.
